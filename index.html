Got Questions

FAQ

Is RunTogether free to download?

Yes — RunTogether is free to download on the App Store. You get a 7-day free trial with full access to all features, including live ranked races, run clubs, and ghost mode. After the trial, you can subscribe weekly ($4.99), monthly ($9.99), or yearly ($49.99). Cancel anytime.

Can I race friends online with RunTogether?

Yes. RunTogether is a live multiplayer running app — you can race friends in real time, create private run clubs with your crew, or jump into public ranked races against other runners worldwide. Everyone's avatar moves as they move, updated every step.

Does RunTogether work on a treadmill?

Yes. RunTogether supports treadmill running with manual speed input — no GPS required. You enter your treadmill speed and the app tracks your movement in real time, powering your live avatar just like outdoor GPS running. You can race other treadmill runners or mix with outdoor runners.

Does RunTogether work on a StairMaster?

Yes. RunTogether has a dedicated StairMaster mode with 20 intensity levels. Set your machine level and race other climbers in real time. It's the only app that turns your StairMaster session into a live multiplayer competition.

How do ranked leagues work?

RunTogether uses a skill-based ranked system with six tiers: Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond, and Champion. Every race earns or costs League Points based on your finish position. You're matched against runners at a similar pace, so the competition is always fair and meaningful.

What is Ghost Mode in RunTogether?

Ghost Mode lets you race a recording of yourself — your personal record (PR) or any previous run. There are also AI Pacers at Bronze through Platinum difficulty if you want a challenge without waiting for other runners. Perfect for solo training sessions.

What iPhone is required for RunTogether?

RunTogether requires iOS 16.0 or later. It's currently available on iPhone only. The app is portrait-locked for safety while running.

What race distances does RunTogether support?

You can race 1 mile, 5K, 10K, half marathon, full marathon, or a custom distance of your choice. Run clubs can set their own distance for group runs.
