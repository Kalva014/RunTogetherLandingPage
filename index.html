Your Avatar Moves as You Move

Run outdoors with live GPS tracking or indoors on a treadmill. Either way, your real movement powers your avatar in real time. You see other runners moving — and they see you.

  • GPS outdoor tracking + full treadmill support
  • Live pace indicators — see who is pushing and who is fading
  • Portrait locked for safety while you run

Ranked Leagues + StairMaster Mode

Climb from Bronze to Champion in skill-based ranked races where every finish earns or costs League Points. Or turn your StairMaster session into a live competition — set your level and race other climbers in real time.

  • Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond, Champion
  • Fair matchmaking based on your actual pace
  • StairMaster: 20 intensity levels, multiplayer leaderboards
